First Time

Sometimes you just have to rip it.

I was asked by a buddy my first year at BYU if I wanted to get a season lift ticket to Sundance and I said sure. I bought all the gear to snowboard and watched like 50 videos on youtube.

My first day up on the mountain we took the lift to the top and I just sent it. We did only blues and blacks that first day. It was learn or fall. And while I did fall a lot I also learned a lot.

Off to Bigger Mountains

Once you're hooked you just want the next bigger run.

Now I had another buddy coming to BYU and he said we had to get to Park City to hit some new runs. We agreed and that was absolutely the right decision.

I learned about my favorite place to board. The backcountry. Nothing better than being the first person to shred through some powder. It's a thrill.
