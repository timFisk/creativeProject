The Flank Steak

The versatile steak.

This is a favorite of mine when I cooking outdoors. I like it becase you can pair it with anything. You want steak and eggs? This'll do it. Want a philly cheese steak? This'll do it. The key is in the preperation.

Before you cook it you have to cut it. I usually go with cubed or thin strips beacuse I find that makes it manageable. Cast iron is supreme when cooking outdoors so get your pan nice and hot. Throw your steak on and get your seasoning out. I always use Lawry's Seasoning Salt, Pepper, and cayenne pepper. Medium rare is the only way to enjoy a steak so be sure to not overcook it. Then enjoy.
